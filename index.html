Our Story

From Passion
to Plate

Cardy's Tacos & Takeaways was born from one young man's determination to do something meaningful — for himself and for his community in Blackheath, Eersterivier.

Ricardo runs his food truck from the gate of Nel Tank on Saxenburg Road, preparing every meal himself with care, creativity, and serious culinary skill. Whether it's a crispy quesadilla, a loaded gatsby, or a saucy pizza — every plate is made fresh and made with love.

This isn't just a takeaway. It's a local legend in the making.
